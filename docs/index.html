Certificate workflow

From sample to COA without confusion.

1

Submit

Product category, batch details, matrix, and requested panels are captured before the sample is accepted.

2

Prepare

Samples are logged, reviewed, weighed, prepared, and assigned to the correct analytical route.

3

Analyze

Instrument data, calibration checks, QC records, and panel outputs are reviewed before release.

4

Certify

Clients receive a clean certificate with sample identity, method notes, panel results, and release-ready formatting.
